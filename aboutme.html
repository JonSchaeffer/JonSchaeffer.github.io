Welcome! I'm Jonathan Schaeffer and I am a student at Allegheny College in the midst of my Junior Year. At Allegheny, I'm studying Music as my Major and Computer Science as my Minor. I am involved with numerous music ensembles on campus such as the Wind Symphony, Wind Ensemble, Jazz Band, Civic Symphony, and Saxophone Ensemble. Recently, I was selected to play in the CBDNA small college honors band where I performed with a group of college students from all over the world at Kansas City, MO. Furthermore, I have been selected to play in the Pennsylvania Intercollegiate Band for the past three years. I am also planning to put on a recital in February which will include works from Glazunov, Corelli, and more.

When I'm not practicing or attending rehersal, I work with the Allegheny ITS department. For the past three years, I have worked alongside fulltime staff in the User Services department and more recently the Technology and Networking Services department. Along with my Computer Science minor, I am learning anything from object oriented programming to configuring, maintaining, and deploying Cisco AP's and switches. I have also worked with Quandarymat over the summer where I learned the 'basics' on Web Development.

I am most thankful for the support of my parents, Shawn and Kris Schaeffer. Without their constant support, I wouldn't be where I am today. My plan is to attend Graduate School for Music Technology or Saxophone Performance. I am also considering the idea to continue in the field of Information Technology or Web Development.
